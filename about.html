About Toji Resturant

Information:

A restaurant managed by a Palestinian family was established in 2007 in the family's old house, which was built in 1956. Nestled in the heart of the community, the restaurant not only serves as a dining venue but also as a living testament to the family's rich heritage and traditions. The historical architecture of the building adds a unique charm, providing guests with a warm and nostalgic atmosphere that feels like a home away from home.
