Welcome to Our Wishlist!

Looking for an unforgettable dining experience?

On this page, we’ve gathered some of our best dishes that we highly recommend you try. Each dish has been carefully selected to ensure you enjoy a memorable dining experience full of exquisite flavors.
Explore our exceptional selections below, and feel free to add them to your wishlist for your next visit.

  • Mixed Grill


    • Description: An unforgettable experience for meat lovers! Our Mixed Grill platter features a selection of the finest grilled meats, including juicy kebabs, tender shish tawook, and marinated beef. Each cut is expertly grilled over charcoal, delivering rich, mouth-watering flavors. Served with freshly baked pita bread and accompanied by side dishes such as crisp salad and creamy tahini, this dish offers a perfect blend of traditional and authentic flavors in every bite. Enjoy a truly exceptional dining experience with our Mixed Grill.



  • Mansaf


    • Description: Experience the heart of Palestinian cuisine with our traditional Mansaf. This iconic dish features tender, slow-cooked lamb simmered in a rich and savory yogurt sauce, served over a bed of fragrant jasmine rice. Garnished with toasted almonds and fresh parsley, it’s accompanied by a side of flatbread for a truly authentic taste. Mansaf is traditionally enjoyed during special occasions and gatherings, making it a perfect choice for a hearty and flavorful meal that brings people together.



  • Maklouba with Chicken


    • Description: Enjoy the authentic flavors of Palestinian cuisine with our traditional Maqluba with chicken. This beloved dish features tender chicken pieces cooked with a medley of vegetables such as eggplant, potatoes, and cauliflower, all layered with aromatic rice. The dish is expertly cooked to blend the rich flavors and spices. Served upside down to reveal the beautiful layers of rice, vegetables, and chicken, it’s garnished with toasted nuts and fresh parsley. Maqluba is perfect for family gatherings and special occasions, offering a delightful and hearty dining experience.
